This is our About page

Capacity Bay, Inc. is an eLearning platform founded in 2014 by Obi Nwachukwu. It aims to empower diverse communities, including veterans, immigrants, and those in technology-deprived areas, by offering practical and accessible online learning and workforce development training. Their services extend globally, addressing substantial unemployment and skills gaps in regions like sub-Saharan Africa. Capacity Bay also provides IT consulting for individuals, businesses, and government agencies. With a focus on simplifying online learning and hands-on training, they prioritize personalized development and group training, making it convenient to learn from home or office.
